Welcome to my website! I’m a Ph.D. Candidate in Developmental and Psychological Science and a Ph.D. Scholar in Data Science at Stanford University, advised by Professors Ben Domingue and Jason Yeatman. My research is generously supported by the Stanford Data Science Fellowship.

I’ve also been fortunate to be supervised by Professors Johnny Zhang, Esther Ulitzsch, Junhao Pan, Xinya Liang, and Edward Ip on particular projects. My academic training spans both psychology and statistics—I hold a M.S. and B.S. in Psychology from Sun Yat-sen University and am pursuing a M.S. in Statistics at Stanford.

Research Interests in Psychometrics

  • Evaluating and improving the generalizability of Structural Equation Models.
  • Identifying model violations in factor analysis.
  • Bayesian Regularization, Mixture Modeling, and Longitudinal Data Analysis.
